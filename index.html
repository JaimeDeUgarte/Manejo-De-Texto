< br>: Elemento vacio

el elemento < br> es un elemento de salto de linea utilizado en poemas mayormente

O’er all the hilltops
Is quiet now,
In all the treetops
Hearest thou
Hardly a breath;
The birds are asleep in the trees:
Wait, soon like these
Thou too shalt rest.
